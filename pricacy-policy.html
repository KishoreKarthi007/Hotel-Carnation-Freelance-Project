Introduction

Welcome to Hotel Carnation's Privacy Policy. At Hotel Carnation, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or make use of our services. By accessing our website and using our services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website or make a reservation:

  1. Personal Information: This includes your name, contact information, and email address when you make a reservation or subscribe to our newsletter.
  2. Reservation Information: We collect information about your stay preferences, room selections, and special requests to provide you with the best service possible.
  3. Usage Data: We may collect information about your interaction with our website, including pages visited, browser type, device information, and IP address.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Reservations: To process and manage your room reservations, including sending confirmations and updates.
  2. Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and address any concerns you may have.
  3. Marketing Communication: With your consent, we may send you promotional offers, newsletters, and updates about our services.
  4. Improvement of Services: We analyze usage data to improve our website, services, and customer experience.
